Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the Quiz App free?

Yes. You can enjoy a wide range of quizzes for free. Premium features may also be available for users who want additional content and advanced learning tools.

2. Do I need to create an account?

You can browse some quizzes without an account, but creating one allows you to save your progress, compete on leaderboards, and earn rewards.

3. Can I play with friends?

Absolutely! Challenge your friends in multiplayer quiz battles and compare scores on the leaderboard.

4. Are new quizzes added regularly?

Yes. Our team continuously updates the platform with fresh questions and new quiz categories.

5. Can I track my performance?

Yes. The app provides detailed statistics, quiz history, accuracy rates, and progress reports.

6. Is the app suitable for students?

Definitely. Students can use the app to prepare for competitive exams, improve subject knowledge, and practice regularly.
